DevMerge dif-murj

noun Emergent co-working venue for developers. Example: My friend was in the zone at our devmerge yesterday.

Why DevMerge?

Surround yourself with other coders and work in stimulating environments. DevMerge exploits peer-pressure as a productivity hack.

How do I start?

Check the map to find places others are working at right now. Co-working spaces, airport terminals, your nearest Starbucks, etc. Go join the merge!

How can I publish my DevMerge?

It just takes a few easy steps.

  1. Sign in with Facebook to get listed on the map.
  2. Check in on Facebook with the #devmerge hashtag.
  3. (Optional) Tag friends in your check in.

Note: It may take a few minutes for new merges to appear on the map.
